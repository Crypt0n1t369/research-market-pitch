Original thesis

Bet intellectual energy on what matters.

The initial idea was a market where contributors stake their intellectual energy on research and idea validation in an open, facilitated, cyclical process.

Pivot

Research is the wedge.

To enter the market, we focus on funded research topics from companies, states, and funds.

Contributor upside

Mine stake by contributing signal.

Researchers earn project stake by producing evidence, critique, validation, synthesis, and useful disagreement.

Mechanism

Iterative open development.

01Capital posts a topic

A funder defines the question, constraints, and reward pool.

02Contributors enter

Selected talent stakes work into research, validation, and critique.

03Facilitated cycles run

The system surfaces evidence, disagreement, blind spots, and next questions.

04Synthesis compounds

Outputs become decision-ready intelligence and seed the next cycle.

Talent network

Universities plus proof-of-work talent.

We collaborate with universities and work with PhD students, master’s students, and exceptional prodigies who have already proven themselves through their work.

Why now

Organizations need better ways to allocate research capital.

We turn open validation into a market: capital chooses questions, talent competes to create signal, and every cycle makes the intelligence layer stronger.
