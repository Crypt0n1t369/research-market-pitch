Investor pitch

A market for useful research.

Turning open idea validation into a capital-deployable research engine.

Original thesis

Bet intellectual energy on what matters.

The first idea was an open market where contributors stake their intellectual energy on research and idea validation.

Work happens in facilitated cycles: propose, critique, validate, synthesize, and improve.

Market-entry pivot

Research as the wedge.

Companies, states, and funds deploy capital on topics they need researched.

Contributors mine stake in projects by producing high-quality validation, evidence, analysis, and synthesis.

Contributor network

Talent with proof of work.

We collaborate with universities and recruit PhD students, master’s students, and exceptional prodigies.

Selection is based on demonstrated work, not credentials alone.

Mechanism

Iterative open development.

  1. 01Capital posts a research topic.
  2. 02Contributors stake work into validation.
  3. 03Facilitated cycles surface evidence and disagreement.
  4. 04Synthesis turns the work into decisions.
  5. 05The next cycle compounds the result.

Outcome

Better research allocation.

A scalable way to fund questions, coordinate talent, and turn open validation into investable intelligence.
