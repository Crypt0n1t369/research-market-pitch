Research Market Investor pitch · v0.1

01 / Thesis

A market for research intelligence.

Capital funds questions. Exceptional contributors mine stake by validating what is true, useful, and investable.

02 / Original idea

The first vision: bet intellectual energy.

We began with a market where contributors could bet their intellectual energy on research and idea validation — openly, iteratively, and in facilitated cycles.

03 / Pivot

Research is the market-entry wedge.

Instead of starting as a broad idea market, we start with funded research topics: companies, states, and funds deploy capital toward questions they need answered.

04 / Product

Deploy capital on topics. Receive decision-grade intelligence.

  • Funders post research topics, constraints, and reward pools.
  • Contributors enter by proving capability and staking work.
  • The platform turns evidence, critique, synthesis, and validation into usable outputs.

05 / Mechanism

Iterative open development.

Question Research Critique Synthesis Next cycle

Every round is facilitated: claims are challenged, evidence is ranked, gaps are exposed, and the next research sprint becomes sharper.

06 / Contributors

Talent mines stake through contribution.

Contributors earn project stake by producing signal: validation, literature maps, expert critique, model comparisons, interviews, experiments, and synthesis.

07 / Network

Universities + proof-of-work talent.

We collaborate with universities and work with PhD students, master’s students, and prodigies who have already proven themselves through their work.

08 / Why it wins

A new primitive for research capital.

Today, research spend is slow, closed, and hard to verify. We make it open, cyclical, competitive, and compounding.
